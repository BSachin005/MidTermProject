Addendum

This interactive story was created as a choose-your-own-adventure game. The process involved crafting a narrative with various choices and consequences, making use of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Creative decisions were made to enhance the user experience, such as adding images to each stage and incorporating a dynamic color scheme.

Challenges were encountered during the implementation, particularly in ensuring that buttons at each stage functioned correctly. Through debugging and careful examination of the JavaScript code, these issues were resolved.

Insights gained during this process include the importance of maintaining a clear and organized structure in the code, as well as the significance of thorough testing to identify and address potential issues.
